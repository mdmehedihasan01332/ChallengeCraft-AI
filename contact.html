Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get started with ChallengeCraft AI?

To get started, simply create an account, set your preferences, and click on "Start a Challenge" on the home page. Our AI will generate a personalized challenge based on your skill level and preferences.

Can I customize the types of challenges I receive?

Yes! You can customize your challenge preferences in your profile settings. You can select preferred categories, difficulty levels, and more to tailor the challenges to your interests.

What if I need help solving a challenge?

Each challenge includes a "Get Hint" button that provides guidance without giving away the solution. You can also reach out to our support team for additional assistance.

How does the points system work?

Points are awarded based on challenge difficulty, completion time, and accuracy of your solution. More difficult challenges and efficient solutions earn higher points, which contribute to your level and ranking on the leaderboard.
